WATCH LIVE TV

Live television is a television production broadcast in real-time, as events happen, in the present. In most cases live programming is not being filmed as it is shown on TV but rather was not rehearsed or edited after filming and is being shown only as it was filmed prior to being aired. Shows broadcast live include newscasts, morning shows, awards shows, sports programs, and, occasionally, episodes of television series.
